What we look for

Blackbear Wisbech pairs intimate lighting with refined sound. We welcome:

  • Acoustic duos, jazz collectives, soul and R&B artists
  • Spoken word, comedy, storytelling evenings
  • Specialty performers that elevate private bookings

If your act sets the right tone for a discerning crowd, we want to meet you.
